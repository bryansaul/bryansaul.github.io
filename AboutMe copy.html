About Me

Born and raised in Barranquitas, I am currently an Industrial & Systems Engineering PhD student at the University of Wisconsin Madison with an interest in expanding my knowledge and skills in Statistics, Machine Learning, Simulation and Operations Research. Before that, I was a student at the University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez where I completed my Bachelor's degree in 2022.

During my undergraduate years at UPRM, I had the opportunity to serve in different student organizations such as the Student Council of the Faculty of Engineering, the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers, Formula SAE Team and an initiative that seeks to mentor other undergraduate students in their academic and professional development known as PLTL. On the other hand, I also had the opportunity to work as a Supply Chain Coop student at Merck & Co. and as a Global Planner for Ethicon LLC.

My motivation to pursue graduate studies materializes from the experiences that I have gathered as a person and from the example that I have followed from mentors and colleagues. As a result, I would like to deepen my knowledge in statistics and machine learning and to explore more about topics related to systems simulation, and optimization with applications to healthcare.
